Understanding how NeuralSync protects your data

NeuralSync collects the following information when you use our platform:

  • Conversation Data: All messages you send to NeuralSync, including user input and bot responses
  • Metadata: Timestamps, message hashes, and session information
  • Usage Analytics: Data about how you interact with our platform
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, and device information
Important: We DO NOT collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To deliver and improve the NeuralSync chatbot experience
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and optimize our platform
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud or malicious activity
  • Communication: To notify you about platform updates or changes to this policy

Your conversation data is never shared with third parties, sold to advertisers, or used for training other AI models without your explicit consent.

NeuralSync employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SHA-256 Hashing: Every conversation is cryptographically hashed for data integrity verification
  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted
  • Database Security: Conversations are stored securely with access controls
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security assessments to identify and fix vulnerabilities
Transparency Note: We believe security should be verifiable. The hashes of all conversations can be independently verified by you, ensuring no tampering has occurred.

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your conversations anytime through your dashboard
  • Export: You can download your complete conversation history with verification hashes
  • Delete: You can request permanent deletion of your data
  • Verification: You can independently verify the authenticity of your conversations using the provided hashes

To exercise these rights, please contact us directly. We typically respond to data requests within 30 days.

NeuralSync uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Only for session management and security
  • No Third-Party Trackers: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking
  • Privacy-First: We prioritize your privacy over advertising and analytics
We believe you have the right to privacy. We're committed to collecting only the minimum data necessary to operate our service.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy here and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of NeuralSync following the posting of revised Privacy Policy terms means that you accept and agree to the changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we're protecting your information.

Last Updated: January 2025
